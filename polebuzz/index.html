About Us

Welcome to San Diego Pole Buzz, a studio focused on imparting the skills of pole dance in the most safe, supportive and empowering way possible. We believe in nurturing everyone's creativity and artistic development as a pole dancer, beyond just gaining all the amazing skills through increased strength and stamina and flexibility for all the pole moves you desire! We offer a warm and positive environment to foster the growth that all dancers want and need. Reach out today to learn more about the classes we offer and how to get involved.
